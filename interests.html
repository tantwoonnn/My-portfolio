Interests & Hobbies

趣味 · The Stuff I Actually Care About

What Takes Up My Time

✦
🎮

Gaming

Games are where I go to switch off but also where I pick up a ton about design, pacing, and world-building without realizing it. From Bloodborne's atmosphere to ULTRAKILL's movement system, I pay attention to how things are made.

🎨

Drawing & Designing

I sketch characters, UI concepts, and random things I want to see exist. Drawing is how I think visually before I open a code editor, I usually have something rough on paper or in my head.

💻

Coding

Mostly web stuff right now HTML, CSS, and C#. I like that moment when something finally works after an hour of debugging. It's frustrating but satisfying in a weird way.

🎵

Music

Lofi hip-hop and jazz rap are my go-to while working. Nujabes, Creepy Nuts, Shing02 the kind of music that doesn't get in the way of thinking. Kanye and Yuika for when I want something with more energy.

📺

Anime & Manga

Attack on Titan, JoJo, JJK, Sakamoto Days I'm into stories with strong world-building and good fight choreography. War of the Adults is underrated. Shangrila Frontier is a love letter to gaming itself.

🎬

Film

I appreciate movies with something to say. Fight Club for its structure, Bullet Train for its style, Rush for how it makes you feel the speed. Sing a Bit of Harmony hit different in ways I wasn't expecting.

🕹

Game Design

More of an interest than a skill right now, but I think about it a lot level design, enemy behavior, UI feedback, how games communicate without words. Eventually this is where I want to actually work.

🌙

Late Night Deep Dives

Reading about game development, watching design breakdowns, falling into rabbit holes about how something was made. I learn a lot at 2am. Not always a good thing, but here we are.
