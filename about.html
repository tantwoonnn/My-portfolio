Hey, I'm Kristan

I'm a 17-year-old from San Fernando, Pampanga currently grinding through my first year of ACT at Clark College. I got into coding through ICT in Senior High and honestly haven't stopped since. I spend most of my free time either building something, drawing, playing games, or watching things that inspire me.

I'm not the type who had everything figured out early I'm figuring it out as I go. Right now I want to be a game developer or a web developer. Maybe both. The overlap is bigger than people think.

I like things that are well-made: good UI, good game design, good music. I notice details. I get annoyed by bad UX. I think caring about craft matters, even when you're just starting out.
