考え · Things I Think About

How I See Things

✦

I'm 18, so I don't have everything figured out. But I do have opinions, and I think about things a lot. These are some of them.

創

On Making Things

I think making something anything is one of the best things a person can do. It doesn't have to be good. It just has to exist. The act of finishing a project, even a small one, teaches you more than a semester of theory.

失

On Failure

I've broken code that worked fine, submitted things I wasn't proud of, and started projects I never finished. But I think that's part of it. You can't get better at something without also being bad at it first.

遊

On Games as Art

People underestimate games. Bloodborne tells a story through architecture. ULTRAKILL teaches rhythm through enemy design. Omori uses mechanics to show how grief works. That's not entertainment that's craft.

夢

On Having Big Dreams

I want to make games for a living and start my own studio someday. People might think that's unrealistic. But I'd rather chase something real and fall short than never try because I was afraid of looking foolish.

道

On the Process

I'm not at the destination yet. I'm not even close. But I'm moving classes, projects, late nights, small improvements. I think that's all you can really ask for at this stage.

間

On Downtime

Resting isn't wasting time. Music, games, doing nothing for a bit it all feeds back into the work eventually. The best ideas usually come when I'm not trying to have them.
