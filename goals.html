The Roadmap

✦

I don't have a 10-year plan written out in a spreadsheet. But I have direction. Here's what I'm working toward — short, mid, and long.

Short-Term · Now to 2 Years
01 Right Now

Graduate from ACT

Finish the Associate in Computer Technology program strong. Not just pass actually understand what I'm learning and come out the other side with real skills.

02 Ongoing

Get Better at Programming

Go deeper in the languages I already know and start building things that are actually useful not just school assignments. Practice by doing real projects.

03 Ongoing

Level Up My Design and Art

Keep drawing consistently. Get better at UI design, composition, and visual storytelling. Learn the principles, not just the tools.

Mid-Term · 3 to 5 Years
04 3–5 Years

Travel to Japan

It's been a goal for a while. I want to experience it firsthand the culture, the architecture, the people. Not just as a tourist but with an appreciation for why it matters to me.

05 3–5 Years

Become Fluent in Japanese

Conversational first, then reading. I want to actually understand what's being said in the media I consume — not rely on subtitles forever. JLPT N3 is the target.

Long-Term · 5+ Years
06 Long Game

Create My Own Game

Ship something. A real game small scope, fully playable, put out into the world. It doesn't have to be huge. It just has to exist and be something I made.

07 Long Game

Start a Game Dev Studio

If the first game works, build on it. Grow a small team, make things together, build something sustainable. The kind of studio I'd want to work at.

08 Life Goals

Have a Family

A wife and kids, eventually. Not in a rush but it's on the list. Build something worth coming home to.
